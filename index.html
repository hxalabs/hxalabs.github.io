Deaf since birth. Builder by nature. Joshua built a coaching business to $245K MRR with his wife. Now building solo with AI, turning that same trust-first approach into tools developers actually need.

He asked one question nobody was asking: why do developers trust tools that can't prove they work? That question became DevSafe and HXA Labs.

No venture capital. No board. No pressure to compromise. Just a founder who builds what he believes in and stands behind his work.

"Every developer I talked to had the same story: they lost code, lost time, or lost trust in a tool that couldn't prove it did what it said."

"Your code. Your backups. Your confidence. Tools that work the way you expect, every single time." That's the standard. Not the upsell.

— Joshua D. Ledbetter, Founder
